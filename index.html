About

I'm Maia, a designer and developer based in Montréal. I'm completing my BFA in Computation Arts at Concordia, concentrating in UX/UI Design and Web Development.

I care about the details: the moment a user hesitates, the affordance they miss, the flow that could be three steps instead of five. Good design is invisible: you only notice it when it's gone.

Outside of screens, I've organized events for 500+ people, led student communities, and like to build things from scratch. I bring that same systems thinking to every design challenge.
